A US military field jacket from the mid-1960s in olive drab cotton sateen. Four patch pockets, original buttons, no alterations.

The Story

Standard Issue, Non-Standard History.

This jacket left the military supply chain sometime in the late 1960s — how, exactly, is unclear. It passed through a thrift store in San Francisco around 1972, judging by the price tag still sewn inside the collar: $2.00.
