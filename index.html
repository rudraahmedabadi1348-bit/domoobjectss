About D.O.M.O.

Things Worth
Keeping.

The Department of Miscellaneous Objects is an archive of the curious, the forgotten, and the beautifully ordinary. We believe every object carries the weight of the hands that held it and the stories that shaped it.

From vintage field equipment to handcrafted curiosities, DOMO exists to give these objects a second life — catalogued, celebrated, and passed on to those who will cherish them most.

This is not just a shop. It is a department. A place of record. A home for things that deserve to be found.
